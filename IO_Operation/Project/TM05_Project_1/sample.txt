The batting parkscores runs by striking the ball bowled at the park wicket with
the park bat, while thebowling and park fielding side tries to prevent this

bowled, when the ball hits parkand dislodges bails, and by the fielding
side park ball after it is hit by the bat, but before it hitsthe park. When ten
